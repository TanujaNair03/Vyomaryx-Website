Focus Areas

Putting intelligence where the risk is.

The most dangerous tasks in modern defence operations share a common thread - they require a human to be present in a place they should not have to be. Vyomaryx builds autonomous systems that change this, using edge AI on uncrewed aerial platforms.

What We Build

Edge AI on autonomous drones.

Everything we build rests on the same foundation: an autonomous uncrewed aerial platform that carries its own intelligence onboard. No operator in the loop. No dependence on a live connection. The drone goes, sees, decides, and reports - on its own.

The tasks we are targeting are ones that are currently done by soldiers in exposed positions, or by manual processes that are too slow for the tempo of modern operations. We are building the systems that automate them.

Our Focus

Drones that take on the tasks soldiers should not have to.

Some of the most critical tasks in field operations - going forward to observe a position, identifying a threat, relaying its location back to command - still put a soldier in harm's way or depend on a manual process that is too slow for the moment.

Vyomaryx builds autonomous uncrewed aerial systems that take these tasks on instead. The intelligence runs onboard, at the edge - so the drone can observe, assess, and report on its own, without an operator in the loop and without depending on a live connection.

The goal

Automate the dangerous, time-critical work that is done manually today - so the drone takes the risk, and the soldier gets the result.

Why It Matters

Automate the task. Protect the soldier.

Every system we set out to build serves one purpose: take work that currently puts a soldier in a dangerous position or relies on a slow manual process, and hand it to an autonomous system that can do it faster and at lower risk.

This is what edge AI on autonomous drones makes possible. Not science fiction - a specific, achievable capability that India's armed forces need and do not yet have at scale.
