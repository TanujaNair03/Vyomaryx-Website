Focus Areas

Putting intelligence where it matters most.

Modern operations place extraordinary demands on the people who carry them out. Vyomaryx builds autonomous uncrewed systems that support those operations - taking on tasks well suited to automation, using edge AI on uncrewed aerial platforms.

What We Build

Edge AI on autonomous platforms.

Everything we build rests on the same foundation: an autonomous uncrewed aerial platform that carries its own intelligence onboard. No operator in the loop, and no dependence on a live connection. The platform observes, assesses, and reports on its own.

The tasks we focus on are ones well suited to automation - work that an autonomous system can take on so that personnel can direct their attention to command and judgement.

Our Focus

Autonomy that extends what a force can do.

Vyomaryx develops autonomous uncrewed aerial systems designed to take on demanding operational tasks independently - observing, assessing, and reporting without continuous human control.

By placing capable intelligence onboard the platform itself, we give forces a way to gather what they need and act on it, while keeping their people in a position of greater safety and oversight.

The goal

Extend the reach of a force and reduce the exposure of its people - so the platform takes on the task, and the operator stays focused on the decision.

Why It Matters

Capability that keeps people one step removed from risk.

Every system we build serves a single purpose: to give forces a capable, autonomous means of carrying out demanding tasks, while keeping their people at a safer distance and in command of the outcome.

This is what edge AI on autonomous platforms makes possible - a capability that modern militaries increasingly need, delivered in a form they can own and trust.
