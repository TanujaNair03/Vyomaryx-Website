01

Vision

How we get there.

03

Focus Areas

Putting intelligence where it matters most.

Modern operations place extraordinary demands on the people who carry them out. Vyomaryx builds autonomous systems that take on the tasks best suited to automation.

F · 01

Autonomous reconnaissance

Reconnaissance is one of the most exposure-heavy tasks in field operations. A soldier must go forward, get eyes on a position, and return - all under threat.

The platform goes instead. It surveys the area, builds a picture of what is there, and returns that intelligence to the operator - without a single soldier having to break cover.

F · 02

Autonomous threat detection

Detecting and responding to a threat still depends on a human observer, a human decision, and a human action - each adding time the situation cannot afford.

The system detects a threat autonomously, makes a determination, and delivers the relevant coordinates back to the ground control centre - closing a loop that was previously slow and human-dependent.

Why it matters

Every system we build serves one purpose: give forces a capable, autonomous way to carry out demanding tasks, while keeping their people at a safer distance and in command of the outcome.
