About Vyomaryx

We build the intelligence that lets machines fly the mission so people don't have to.

Our story

Vyoma — व्योम — is the Sanskrit word for the sky, the open expanse where everything we make belongs. Vyomaryx began with a simple conviction: that India's forces should operate autonomy they fully own, understand, and control — not black boxes shipped from somewhere else.

We started as a small group of perception researchers, flight-controls engineers, and former operators frustrated by the same gap. Drones could fly, but they couldn't think — the moment a link dropped or a signal was jammed, the mission stalled. So we set out to move the intelligence onto the aircraft itself.

Today we build the autonomy layer for uncrewed aerial systems: perception, navigation, and coordination that work at the edge, in the conditions that matter, sovereign by design.
