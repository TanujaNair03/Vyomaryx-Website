About Vyomaryx

Intelligence, onboard.

Our story

Vyoma - व्योम - is the Sanskrit word for the sky, the expanse where everything we build operates. Vyomaryx was founded on a clear principle: that autonomous systems entrusted with critical operations should be fully owned, understood, and controlled by the forces that deploy them.

The company was founded by two people who each bring one half of the problem. One served twenty years in the Indian Army, with direct experience of the situations where personnel are placed at risk to carry out tasks a machine could perform instead. The other is an engineer working in the AI that makes such machines possible. Together, that is Vyomaryx.

The challenge we returned to again and again was a fundamental one: uncrewed platforms could fly, but they could not operate independently. Vyomaryx exists to place genuine intelligence onboard the platform itself.
